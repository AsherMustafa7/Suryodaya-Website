Welcome to the Night

In the depths of darkness, a single light can transform everything. Our street lamp stands as a beacon of hope, casting warm light into the void and creating a sanctuary of comfort and safety.

Watch as it responds to your presence, flickering to life with each interaction, breathing personality into the stillness of the night.

About 1.50 lakh solar street lights have been installed in the state by Uttar Pradesh New and Renewable

Energy Development Agency under various schemes of the Central/State Government. Uttar Pradesh Suryoday Mobile App has been developed by the Uttar Pradesh Government in collaboration with UPLC for the regular maintenance and quick repair by identifying non-functional lights.
